Here is some information about me.

  • My name is Alex Robinson and I live in Columbia Missouri. My background experience is over five years of law enforcement service including work as a security officer at a local college. I have always found success and satisfaction when helping people and with my interest in the use of technology to advance learning I decided to integrate both with a degree in Information Science and Learning Technologies, focusing in Learning Systems Design and Development. My undergraduate degree in education and business has provided me with a strong educational pedagogy. Through law enforcement I have helped people in times of crisis, and with this degree, I plan to pursue work that will help other law enforcement personnel have better training to ensure they are at their best when out in the field.

The three primary goals are associated to competencies required by the University of Missouri. Those goals are:

  1. Goal 1: To provide students with experiences to develop theory-grounded and research-based competencies for the innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable design/development and management of technologies for learning opportunities and systems.
  2. Goal 2: To provide students with experiences to develop skills in data collection, analysis and evaluation for reflecting on and applying to the design/development of learning opportunities and systems.
  3. Goal 3: To provide students with experiences to become socially responsible, reflective/sensitive on the interaction of technologies & society and act ethically in response to current and future challenges of emerging technologies for learning.
